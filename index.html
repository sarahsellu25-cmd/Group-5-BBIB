Who We Are

Education Built for Sierra Leone

EduSL is a free digital education platform connecting Sierra Leonean students with quality learning materials, interactive lessons, and community support regardless of location.

  • Free access for all registered students
  • Curriculum aligned with the WAEC & NPSE syllabuses
  • Mobile-friendly works on basic smartphones
  • Covers primary, secondary, and tertiary levels
  • Resources in English and Krio
